How I Deliver NHS Digital Transformation

🏥 Patient‑Centred, Clinically Safe Delivery

Digital change in the NHS must protect patient safety and clinical workflows. I work closely with CCIOs, CNIOs, clinical safety officers, and operational teams to ensure transformation is safe, compliant, and aligned to DCB 0129/0160 standards.

📘 Governance That Supports Pace and Assurance

I establish governance frameworks that provide clarity, risk control, and auditability — while enabling delivery teams to move at pace. This includes integrated planning, RAID discipline, and structured reporting.

🧩 Aligning Clinical, Operational, and Technical Teams

NHS transformation requires alignment across estates, IT, clinical services, operations, and external suppliers. I ensure all parties are coordinated, informed, and working toward shared outcomes.
