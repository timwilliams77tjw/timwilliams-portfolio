Cloud Transformation Programme Manager

Leading complex cloud transformation programmes that balance pace, security, governance, and business change — ensuring organisations adopt cloud safely, predictably, and without disruption.

Delivering Cloud Adoption Without Disruption

Cloud transformation succeeds when it’s treated as a business change programme, not a technical migration. I specialise in delivering cloud programmes that align technology, people, and process — ensuring adoption is secure, compliant, and operationally ready.

From landing zones and identity foundations to application migration and modern workplace transformation, I bring structure, clarity, and leadership to cloud initiatives.

How I Deliver Cloud Transformation

☁️ Cloud as a Business Change Programme

Successful cloud adoption requires alignment across security, operations, architecture, finance, and end‑users. I ensure the programme is structured around outcomes, not technology alone.

🔐 Secure‑by‑Design Foundations

I lead programmes that establish identity, access, landing zones, governance, and compliance controls aligned to ISO 27001, NCSC, and organisational policy.

📘 Predictable Delivery Through Governance

Using APM, Agile, and integrated planning, I build governance that provides clarity and control while enabling pace and flexibility.

🧩 Managing Complex Dependencies

Cloud transformation touches every part of the organisation. I coordinate architecture, security, operations, suppliers, and business stakeholders to ensure alignment and momentum.

📈 Measurable Outcomes

• Secure landing zone established
• Identity and access modernisation
• Application migration and remediation
• Operational readiness and service transition
• Improved resilience, scalability, and cost control

Cloud transformation is most successful when governance, security, and delivery discipline come together. That’s where I focus.
