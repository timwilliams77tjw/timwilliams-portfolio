Data Protection Statement

How we comply with UK GDPR and protect personal data.

1. Data Controller

TWC Ltd, Birmingham, United Kingdom.

2. Data We Process

Includes contact details, enquiry information and technical usage data.

3. Why We Process Data

To respond to enquiries, deliver services, improve the site, ensure security and meet legal obligations.

4. Lawful Bases

Consent, legitimate interests, contractual necessity and legal obligation.

5. Sharing

We may share data with hosting providers, analytics tools, communication systems and legal authorities.

6. Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, objection, portability or withdrawal of consent.

8. Security

We use HTTPS, access controls, secure email handling and regular reviews.

9. International Transfers

We do not intentionally transfer data outside the UK. Third‑party tools must comply with UK GDPR safeguards.
